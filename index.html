How LogiCheck AI Works

Our four-step pipeline continuously processes, extracts, and verifies medical claims.

01

Extract Speech

We download and extract the audio track from the provided video content securely.

02

Convert to Text

AI speech recognition converts the spoken audio into an accurate text transcript.

03

Detect Claims

Natural Language Processing analyzes the text to identify specific healthcare claims.

04

Verify Evidence

Claims are crossed-referenced with trusted sources like the WHO and CDC to determine validity.

Advanced Intelligence for Reliable Health Information

LogiCheck AI is built to combat the spread of medical misinformation online. By analyzing video content at scale, we empower users with transparency and trusted medical facts.

  • Speech-to-text models convert video audio swiftly.
  • Natural Language Processing contextualizes spoken language.
  • Semantic embeddings group related medical concepts together.
  • Evidence retrieval queries our curated, authoritative health database.
